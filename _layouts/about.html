About Elf on the Run

What started as a small idea on a run five years ago has snowballed into a group of over 250 people coming together for an event called Elf on the Run. Spearheaded by the Head Elf, Victoria Ten Broeck, the group helps spread holiday cheer to local charities through a combination of donations and service.

 

Every year, Elf on the Run serves the Savannah area in 12 jolly ways to make the season brighter by collecting items for various local charities and coming together in service for our neighbors.

Elf mission

Making community engagement on a local level fun

Elf vision

Mobilizing the power of volunteers and the generosity of local businesses to build sustainable change
